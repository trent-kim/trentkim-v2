Hello, I'm Trent!

Born in Jeonju, South Korea, currently based in New Jersey, I'm an interdisciplinary designer who loves finding innovative and creative ways to tell stories through web design.

In my personal practice, I explore themes of transnational and transracial identity, healing, and language as they relate to my experience as a Korean adoptee. Most recently, I have been writing for an upcoming, collaborative book called Roots: Korean Diaspora.

In my free time, I enjoy taking photos, journaling, and cooking nice meals for myself.
