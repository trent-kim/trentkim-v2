Hello, I'm Trent!

I’m a digital designer based in New Jersey, currently freelancing for Stardust. I most enjoy projects that find creative ways to navigate mundane experiences, tell stories, and facilitate connection through the web.

In my personal practice, I explore themes of transnational and transracial adoption, family, loss, and healing. Check out this collaborative art book project I am a part of, Roots: Korean Diaspora.

When I want to slow down, I enjoy walking, taking photos, journaling, and cooking.
